# Welcome to the Admin Manual

Welcome to the **Alphasuits Admin Panel** — your complete control center for managing your online clothing store. This manual will walk you through every feature of the admin panel in simple, step-by-step detail so you can confidently manage your products, orders, customers, and site settings.

---

## Who Is This Manual For?

This manual is designed for **store administrators** — the people responsible for:

- Adding and editing products
- Uploading product images
- Managing inventory and stock levels
- Processing customer orders and bookings
- Configuring the website appearance and settings
- Managing users and their roles

Whether you are a first-time admin or need a quick refresher, each section of this manual provides clear instructions with screenshots to guide you.

---

## How the Admin Panel Is Organized

The admin panel is accessed through the sidebar on the left side of the screen. Here is a quick overview of each section:

| Section | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | View revenue, orders, active users, recent bookings, and analytics at a glance |
| **Users** | See all registered users, their roles (Admin/User), and manage their accounts |
| **Products** | Add new products, manage categories, update stock, and create discount coupons |
| **Sales** | View and manage all customer orders and their statuses |
| **Bookings** | Manage appointment bookings, record body measurements for custom tailoring |
| **Reviews** | Read and moderate customer reviews |
| **Newsletter** | Manage newsletter subscribers |
| **Settings** | Configure homepage components, slider images, events, filters, and global messages |

---

## Quick Start Checklist

If you are setting up your store for the first time, follow these steps in order:

1. **Create Categories** — Go to **Products → Category** and add your product categories (e.g., "Long Sleeve", "Luxury Coat")
2. **Add Products** — Go to **Products → Add Products** and create your first product with images, description, pricing, and variants
3. **Configure Filters** — Go to **Settings → Filters** and set up category and color filters so customers can easily browse your store
4. **Set Up Slider** — Go to **Settings → Slider** and add homepage banner images
5. **Configure Components** — Go to **Settings → Components** to control which sections appear on your homepage
6. **Set a Welcome Message** — Go to **Settings → Messages** to display a global announcement on your site
